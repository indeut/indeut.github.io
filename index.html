Who are we

Indeut UG

We are Indeut UG, based in Germany, we do trade, distributorship and manufacturing of several goods. Indeut UG was set up by Kashyap Chawda and Rakesh Nandi with the focus of providing ease in doing business.

Trade, Distribution and Manufacturing
